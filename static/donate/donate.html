Donations

I follow the Value 4 Value model. In a nutshell, that means if you get value from this site, we ask you give back value.

Value comes in many forms. For example:

  • A monetary donation
  • Leaving a comment
  • Correcting a mistake
  • Sharing a post on social media

While we do hope for donations to help offset the costs of running this site, time spent, or physical material needs, I don't expect everyone to leave a donation. Just the knowledge that others find my site useful or enjoy my weird musings is payment in itself.
